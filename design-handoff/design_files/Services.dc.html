01

Your digital foundation

Before anything else, these two things need to exist. Without them, every other system is built on sand.

{{ icGlobe }}

A website that works

The problem

You have a website. It's old, it's slow, and every time someone asks for the URL you feel a small wave of embarrassment. Or you don't have one at all, and you're relying on a Facebook page from 2019.

What we build

A clean, fast, mobile-first site — properly coded, not thrown together on a page builder. Loads fast, looks right on every device, and is built to turn visitors into enquiries rather than just exist.

After it's live

You can send people to your website without bracing yourself. Enquiries come in through a form that actually works. New visitors land on something that makes the business look real.

{{ icShield }}

GDPR-compliant data capture

The problem

You collect names, emails and phone numbers — at the till, on a booking form, at the door — and then do nothing with them, because you're not sure you're allowed to or they're in a spreadsheet nobody touches.

What we build

A compliant, dual-consent contact system built into the touchpoints you already have — booking confirmation, receipt, web journey. Every contact collected properly and stored somewhere you can actually use.

After it's live

You own a real marketing list. You can contact your customers without worrying about GDPR. Every other system we build — campaigns, review requests, birthday messages — runs on this foundation.

Your customer engine

{{ icMail }}

Enquiry capture & follow-up

The problem

A lead comes in through the website, a missed call, or a Google message. Nobody gets back to them for three hours — or at all — because the day got busy. They've already booked somewhere else.

What we build

Automatic capture of enquiries from every channel — web form, missed call, Google message. An instant, personal first response. Routing by venue or service. A follow-up sequence that doesn't feel like spam.

After it's live

Every enquiry gets an immediate, personal response, even when you're mid-job. Your leads stop going cold. You stop finding out three days later that someone who wanted to spend money didn't.

{{ icStar }}

Review generation & response

The problem

You have 23 Google reviews, the most recent from 14 months ago. Your competitor across the road has 280. You know reviews matter — you just never remember to ask, and nobody follows through.

What we build

Automatic review requests by SMS or email after a visit. Happy customers go straight to Google; unhappy ones are caught privately so you can fix it first. Responses auto-drafted for your approval, or fully hands-off.

After it's live

Your review count grows on its own. Your rating climbs. New customers searching for what you offer see a business that's clearly active and well-regarded. Problems get caught before they go public.

Your AI layer

For the businesses where the phone never stops, or the same questions get asked forty times a week.

{{ icChat }}

AI chatbot

The problem

The same ten questions get asked on your website every day. What are your hours? Do you do X? How much is Y? Someone on your team answers them. They shouldn't have to.

What we build

A chatbot trained on your own business — services, pricing, FAQs, hours, booking process. Lives on your website and WhatsApp. Not a generic off-the-shelf bot — built on your information, for your customers.

After it's live

Routine questions get answered around the clock without anyone touching them. Staff spend their time on things that need a human. Customers get an answer immediately instead of waiting on an email.

{{ icPhone }}

AI receptionist

The problem

The phone rings constantly. Half the calls are things any member of staff could handle — bookings, prices, directions, hours. But someone still has to answer, and that someone is usually pulled away from something more important.

What we build

A capable AI that handles phone calls directly — takes bookings, answers real questions, and only puts a call through to a human when it genuinely needs to. Built and run by Charlie, one of the Stacksmith founders.

After it's live

The phone still rings. It just doesn't need you to answer it every time. Staff attention goes to what needs a person. The AI handles the volume; the humans handle the exceptions.
